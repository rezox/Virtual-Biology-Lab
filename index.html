Virtual-Biology-Lab by VBL

Virtual laboratory in which students learn the mechanics of mitosis, or cell division, through computer simulation.

This virtual lab simulates lab exercises that are performed by students who are taking general or upper level biology courses. Mitosis is the continuous process that a parent cell undergoes to divide and create two identical cells, which are often called daughter cells. Scientists have divided the process into several discrete phases(usually four to six), each characterized by important events.
